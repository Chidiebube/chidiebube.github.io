ABOUT

Hi! this is AMOS, Chidiebube and he’s called “Bube” by his friends. Currently based in Lagos, Nigeria but operates within diverse time zones. Chidiebube works as a Lead DevOps engineer at Andela. Andela invests in Africa's most talented developers and integrates them into the world's best tech companies. At Andela, he helps nurture the next generations of African Leaders especially in the DevOps space.
He previously worked at Shopp!ng, where he leveraged various cloud technologies to provision, secure, connect and run the company's infrastructure in an agile way. Having worked in various startups, I wear other hats like Product Managment and Quality Assurance when the need arises. Chidiebube strongly values team collaboration, community building, and life long learning.
Outside his day to day job, Chidiebube supports the community by speaking at tech conferences in Lagos, blogging about devops and building open source tools with golang. He organizes Hashicorp User Group Lagos where DevOps and Infrastructure Engineers meet to share knowledge and learn DevOps best practices.
Offline, Chidiebube devotes time to cooking and he loves eating good food :). Chidiebube also makes out time to help under prviledged communities.
